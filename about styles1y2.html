Mission

To support young athletes living in Chicago's northside neighborhoods, who have an interest in learning and playing soccer, with opportunities to learn and practice skills related to the game of sccer, specifically those skills around team cooperation and good sportsmanship

Vision

The Northside Youth Soccer League aspires to develop strong, well-rounded, and mindful athletess through the building of character, self-discipline, and leadership

General information

The Northside Youth Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residin in Chicago's northside neighborhoods an environment in which to learn and play soccer. To be a member of NYSL, you must be between the ages of 4 - 12 and resie in a Chicago northside neighborhood. NYSL is ran by a small full-time staff, and relie on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league members.
